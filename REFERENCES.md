# Reference Notes

**Status: Orientation references, not evidentiary proof.**

This file collects references that may help readers understand the general background, conventional terminology, and neighboring fields related to TCP, Closure Phase Ψ, and DDP.

These references are **not** presented as evidence that TCP, Closure Phase Ψ, or DDP are true.  
They are not proof of the framework.  
They are not authority shields.

They are included only to show:

- common usage of specification language;
- adjacent engineering and conformance practices;
- general AI risk and explainability vocabulary;
- the public-archive and licensing conventions used by this repository;
- background material for the Umami Gap analogy;
- general-language references that help explain why certain terms are separated.

The framework itself remains a public specification archive.  
The proper evaluation mode is **conformance**, not belief.

---

## How These References Should Be Used

Allowed use:

- to understand the general meaning of neighboring terms;
- to compare this repository’s terminology with existing public vocabulary;
- to explain why this repository uses specification-oriented language;
- to support ordinary background statements such as “MUST / SHOULD / MAY are common specification keywords”;
- to show that the repository follows public documentation and licensing conventions.

Prohibited use:

- using these references as proof that TCP / Ψ / DDP are correct;
- using them to imply endorsement by the cited institutions;
- using them as an authority shield;
- using them to bypass the repository’s sealed-domain boundaries;
- using them to connect the framework to superiority scoring, manipulation, or full structuralization of emotion.
